Delete Your Ventra Account

Ventra is committed to giving you full control over your data. You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Ventra account, please send an email from the address registered on your account to:

Email: qpage369@gmail.com
Subject: Delete My Ventra Account
Body: Please include your account email and display name so we can verify ownership.

We will process your request within 7 business days and email you a confirmation when complete.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon receiving your verified deletion request, the following data is permanently deleted from our systems:

  • Your account (email, display name, profile photo, push notification token, bio)
  • All posts you have created
  • All replies you have written
  • All likes and reactions you have given
  • Any other personal data linked to your account

What Data Is Retained

For legal compliance and abuse-prevention purposes, the following may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion:

  • Server logs containing your IP address (anonymised after 30 days)
  • Reports of policy violations submitted against your account, if any

After 90 days, all retained data is permanently and irreversibly deleted.

Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at qpage369@gmail.com.
